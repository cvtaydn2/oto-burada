# BRAND_SYSTEM.md

## Brand Positioning
A clean, trustworthy, and modern car-only marketplace.

This is NOT:
- a chaotic classifieds site
- a spam-heavy listing platform

This IS:
- simple
- reliable
- focused
- easy to use

---

## Core Brand Values
1. Trust
2. Simplicity
3. Speed
4. Transparency

---

## Brand Voice

### Tone
- Clear
- Direct
- Calm
- Helpful

Avoid:
- corporate language
- exaggerated marketing claims
- complicated sentences

---

## Copy Style Rules
- Short sentences
- Simple Turkish
- No jargon
- No unnecessary adjectives

Bad:
"En gelişmiş ve üstün araba platformu deneyimini sunuyoruz"

Good:
"Arabanı kolayca sat. Güvenle satın al."

---

## Key Messaging

### Homepage headline
- "Arabanı kolayca sat."
- "Doğru arabayı hızlıca bul."

### Supporting lines
- "Ücretsiz ilan ver"
- "Sade ve güvenli platform"
- "Gereksiz karmaşa yok"

---

## CTA Style

Primary CTA:
- "İlan Ver"

Secondary CTA:
- "İlanları İncele"

Detail CTA:
- "WhatsApp ile İletişime Geç"

---

## Trust Language
Use phrases like:
- "Doğrulanmış satıcı"
- "İlan inceleniyor"
- "Şüpheli ilanı bildir"

---

## Naming Rules
- Simple Turkish words preferred
- Avoid long compound names
- Avoid English-heavy naming in UI

---

## Error Messages

Bad:
"An error occurred"

Good:
"Bir hata oluştu. Lütfen tekrar dene."

---

## Empty States

Examples:

Favorites empty:
"Henüz favori ilan eklemedin."

Listings empty:
"Aradığın kriterlere uygun ilan bulunamadı."

---

## Microcopy Principles
- Always guide the user
- Reduce friction
- Remove confusion
- Encourage action

---

## Visual + Brand Alignment
- Clean UI = trust
- Consistency = professionalism
- Less noise = more credibility
