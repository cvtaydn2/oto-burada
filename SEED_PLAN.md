# SEED_PLAN.md

This file defines how to generate realistic initial data for the platform.

---

## Goal
Ensure the platform does NOT feel empty at launch.

---

## Minimum Data Targets

Before launch:

- 50 listings minimum
- 10 different brands
- 3+ cities
- 2–3 listings per model

---

## Listing Distribution

Mix:
- low price
- mid range
- high price

Include:
- clean cars
- slightly damaged cars
- urgent sale listings

## Image Strategy

Use:
- real car photos (stock acceptable for MVP)
- consistent aspect ratio
- minimum 3 images per listing

---

## Seller Profiles

Create:
- 1 admin user
- 5 normal users

Distribute listings across users.

---

## Data Realism Rules

- Avoid fake-looking data
- Keep prices realistic
- Keep mileage realistic
- Keep combinations logical

Bad:
2022 model + 300000 km
